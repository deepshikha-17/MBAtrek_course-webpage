Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility criteria for this Product Management Program by CII?

Any student or corporate professional who is eager to crack Product Management Interviews can join this course.

What certificate will I receive upon program completion?

Upon the completion of this certification program, you will be awarded a Certificate in Product Management by CII that holds global recognition to create and sustain an environment conducive to the development of India, partnering industry and government alike through advisory and consultative processes. 


What are the prerequisites of this course?

You should be able to understand the basics of Product Management. CII x MBAtrek provides a FREE PM Foundation course that can help you get introduced to the Product Management.

What sort of interviews will I be able to crack after this course?

After this course, you will be able to crack entry to mid-senior roles in Product Management for multiple profiles such as Business Analyst, Product Analyst, Product Manager, Product Owner, Associate Product Manager, Product Marketing Manager, and Senior PM.

How will live classes happen during the 28 days Product Management program?

The program will have 16 live classes across 4 weekends (Sat & Sun: 10:15 AM to 11:45 AM, 12:00 PM to 01:30 PM) and 10 classes over weekdays - 08:00 PM to 09:30 PM. Overall, there will be 40+ hours of live classes.

What additional support will I get apart from the live classes?

During the program, there will be multiple engagement activities on our community to support you with PM concepts, PM Interview questions, and projects. You will regularly work with your peers and experts to create a project by the end of 4 weeks.

Will you help me build my resume and portfolio?

Our team will take dedicated sessions on resume and portfolio development. We will also train you on two tools - Figma and Wix to help you develop portfolio and flaunt it to the recuiters.

Is this course fully online or do I have to attend classes in person?

This course is delivered in a hybrid manner as a combination of live and recorded sessions. So, theres no need to show up to a classroom in person. You can access your lectures, readings and assignments anytime from anywhere via the web or your smartphone. The course is accessible for one year.
